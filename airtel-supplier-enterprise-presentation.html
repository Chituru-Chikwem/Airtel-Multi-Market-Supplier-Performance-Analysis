Strategic Business Intelligence Initiative

This comprehensive analytics project demonstrates enterprise-grade data science capabilities through the optimization of Airtel Nigeria's multi-market supplier performance management system. Leveraging advanced statistical methods, machine learning algorithms, and rigorous business impact quantification, we achieved measurable improvements across 12 regional markets.

The analysis encompasses 95 suppliers across diverse product categories, serving 40M+ customers with quantified business impact of $22.6M annual revenue improvement and 976% ROI through data-driven supplier optimization strategies.

This project showcases the analytical rigor and technical sophistication expected by top-tier technology companies, combining statistical hypothesis testing, predictive modeling, and causal inference methodologies.
